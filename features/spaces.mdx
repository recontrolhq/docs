---
title: "Spaces"
description: "Isolated workspaces that contain all financial data for a person or group."
---

A space is the top-level container for all financial activity in Recontrol. Every account, transaction, and balance record belongs to exactly one space. Spaces provide complete isolation — data in one space is entirely separate from data in any other.

## Personal space

When you sign up, Recontrol creates a **Personal** space for you automatically. This is your default workspace. You can begin adding accounts and recording transactions immediately.

Your Personal space cannot be deleted. It is yours alone — no one else can be invited to it unless you explicitly create a shared space for that purpose.

## Shared spaces

You can create additional spaces and invite other people to join them. Shared spaces are designed for:

- **Households** — track joint expenses and shared accounts together
- **Partnerships** — manage finances for a business or project with co-founders
- **Groups** — split and track expenses with flatmates, travel companions, or teams

Every member of a shared space can see and interact with the space's data according to the [role and permissions](/features/access-control) they have been assigned.

## Space isolation

Spaces are fully isolated from each other:

- An account in Space A is invisible to members of Space B (unless someone is a member of both).
- A user's access level in one space says nothing about their access in another space.
- Roles and permissions are always space-scoped — they apply only within the space they were defined.

This makes it safe to manage a personal space and a shared household space from the same account, with no data leaking between them.

## Creating and managing spaces

- **Create** a new space from the Settings tab. You are automatically assigned the admin role.
- **Invite** people to a space using their email address or phone number. See [Access control](/features/access-control).
- **Delete** a space only if you are its admin and it is not your default Personal space. Deleting a space permanently removes all accounts and transactions within it.

<Warning>
Deleting a space is permanent and irreversible. All accounts, transactions, and balance history within that space are deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

## Relationships

- A space is created by one user (the owner), who is automatically an admin.
- A space has many [Accounts](/features/accounts), including one automatically-created Unspecified account.
- A space has many [Transactions](/features/transactions).
- A space has many [Memberships](/features/access-control) — the users who belong to it and their roles.
- A space has many pending [Invites](/features/access-control) — people who have been invited but have not yet joined.
