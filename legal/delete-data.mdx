This page explains how to request deletion of your Recontrol account and all personal data associated with it.

This page is accessible without logging in. If you are unable to access the app, you can submit a deletion request by email using the instructions below.

---

<Warning>
**Deletion is permanent and irreversible.**

We do not maintain backups of user data. Once your deletion request is processed, your data cannot be recovered by you or by us under any circumstances.
</Warning>

---

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is **permanently and completely removed** from our systems:

- Your profile: email address, display name, and phone number
- All financial accounts you created across any Space
- All transactions you recorded across any Space
- All Spaces you created (and all data within those Spaces)
- Your membership in all shared Spaces
- Your role assignments
- Your settings and currency preferences
- All authentication tokens and session data

**Nothing is retained.** We do not keep any backup copy of your data. Recontrol stores all data in India (AWS ap-south-1, Mumbai) and there is no secondary copy.

---

## What is NOT deleted

In rare cases required by applicable Indian law, we may retain minimal records — for example, records required by a court order or law enforcement process. We will not retain any data beyond what is legally mandated, and we will never retain data for business purposes after account deletion.

---

## Method 1: Delete from within the app (recommended)

Deleting from within the app is immediate.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the **Settings** tab at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to account options">
    Scroll to the bottom of the Settings screen and find **Delete Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm deletion">
    Read the confirmation prompt carefully. Tap **Delete Account** to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately. You will be signed out and returned to the login screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**If you are the sole admin of a shared Space**, you must either transfer admin ownership to another member or delete the Space before account deletion can complete. The app will prompt you through this step.
</Note>

---

## Method 2: Email request (if you cannot access the app)

If you are unable to log into the app, you can request deletion by email. Email requests are processed within **30 days** of receipt.

**Send an email to:** [support@recontrol.money](mailto:support@recontrol.money?subject=Account%20deletion%20request)

**Subject line:** `Account deletion request`

**Include in your email:**
- The email address associated with your Recontrol account
- A clear statement that you want your account and all associated data permanently deleted

We may ask you to verify your identity before processing the request to protect your data from unauthorised deletion.

---

## Processing time

| Method | Processing time |
|---|---|
| In-app deletion | Immediate |
| Email request | Within 30 days of receipt |

## Requesting written confirmation

You may request written confirmation that your data has been deleted. Email [support@recontrol.money](mailto:support@recontrol.money) after your deletion is complete and ask for confirmation. We will provide it within a reasonable timeframe.
