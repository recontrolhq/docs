## 7. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data is transmitted over TLS/HTTPS
- **Encryption at rest:** Data stored in our databases is encrypted at rest using AES-256, provided by AWS
- **Secure credential storage:** On mobile devices, authentication tokens are stored using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore via Expo Secure Store)
- **JWT authentication:** All API requests are authenticated using signed JSON Web Tokens
- **Role-based access control:** Collaborative Spaces enforce granular permissions
- **Input validation:** All API requests are validated using Protocol Buffer schema validation (protovalidate)
- **Access controls:** Internal access to user data is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security breach, please contact us immediately at support@recontrol.money.

### 7.1 Data breach notification

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to users, we will notify affected users without undue delay and comply with applicable notification requirements under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

## 10. Account deletion and data erasure

### 10.1 How to delete your account

You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time:

- **In-app:** Navigate to **Settings → Delete Account** within the App
- **Web form:** Visit [https://recontrol.money/data/delete](https://recontrol.money/data/delete)
- **Email:** Send a deletion request to support@recontrol.money

### 10.2 What gets deleted

<Warning>
**Deletion is permanent and irreversible. We do not maintain backups of user data. Once your deletion request is processed, your data cannot be recovered by you or by us under any circumstances.**
</Warning>

Upon processing your deletion request, we permanently delete:

- Your user account and profile information (email, display name, phone number)
- All financial accounts you created
- All transactions you recorded
- Your currency preferences and other settings
- Your Space memberships and role assignments
- Session tokens and authentication data

### 10.3 Processing timeframe

In-app deletions are processed immediately. Email requests are processed within **thirty (30) days** of receipt.

### 10.4 Data that may be retained

After account deletion, we may retain minimal data only where required by applicable Indian law (for example, fraud or abuse records required by law enforcement). We will not retain any data beyond what is legally mandated.

### 10.5 Written confirmation

You may request written confirmation that your data has been deleted by emailing support@recontrol.money.

### 10.6 Effect on shared Spaces

- Your membership will be removed from all Spaces
- If you are the sole administrator of a Space, you must transfer ownership or delete the Space before account deletion can complete
