## Delete your account

If you want to permanently delete your account and all associated data, follow the instructions on the dedicated deletion page.

<Card title="Delete your data" icon="trash" href="/data/delete">
  Step-by-step instructions for deleting your account from within the app, or submitting an email request. Deletion is permanent — there are no backups.
</Card>

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is permanently and irreversibly removed from our servers:

- Your user profile
- All financial accounts and transactions across all spaces
- All space memberships and any spaces you own
- All settings and preferences

We do not retain backups. Once deletion is confirmed, your data cannot be recovered by you or by us.

## Only admin of a shared space?

Before deleting your account, you must either transfer admin ownership to another member or delete the shared space entirely. The app will prompt you through this during the account deletion flow.
