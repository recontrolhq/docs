## Is Recontrol free?

Yes. Recontrol is currently free to download and use. If paid features are introduced in the future, you will be given at least 30 days' notice and will never be charged unless you explicitly opt in.

## Does Recontrol connect to my bank?

Not currently. Recontrol is a manual finance tracker — all data is entered by you. It does not ask for bank credentials or access your accounts. Automatic transaction tracking via bank or aggregator APIs is not available yet.

## Is my financial data private?

Yes. Your data is stored encrypted on servers in Mumbai, India (AWS ap-south-1). We do not sell your data, do not run advertising, and do not use any client-side analytics or tracking SDK. See our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy) for the full picture.

## How do I create a shared space for my household?

Go to **Settings** in the app and tap **Create Space**. Give the space a name, then use **Invite Members** to add people by their email address or phone number. Invited members can accept immediately if they have an account, or when they sign up.

## Can I use Recontrol in multiple currencies?

You can set a preferred display currency for each space. Recontrol does not perform currency conversion — it displays the figures you enter in the currency you select. You are responsible for entering amounts in the correct currency for each account.

## What happens to my data if I delete my account?

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All data associated with your account — including your profile, all financial accounts, all transactions, all space memberships, and all settings — is deleted from our servers. We do not retain backups. Once deletion is complete, your data cannot be recovered by you or by us under any circumstances.

See [Delete your data](/data/delete) for full details and step-by-step instructions.

## I am the only admin of a shared space. Can I still delete my account?

Before deleting your account, you must either transfer admin ownership to another member or delete the space entirely. The app will prompt you through this process during the account deletion flow.

## I forgot my password. How do I log in?

On the login screen, tap **Forgot password** and enter your email address. You will receive a password reset link. If you signed up with **Sign in with Apple**, use that option on the login screen — no password is required.
