## Full Description (max 4,000 characters)

*Indexed for search by Google Play. Use natural keyword integration throughout.*

```
Recontrol is the finance tracker that puts you in control of your money. Track every account, record every transaction, and see your complete financial picture — whether you manage your finances solo or together with a partner, family, or team.

TRACK ALL YOUR ACCOUNTS IN ONE PLACE
Create and manage accounts for everything you own or owe: bank accounts, credit cards, cash wallets, investments, loans, and more. Organize them by type — assets, liabilities, income, expense, and equity — and always know exactly where your money stands. Update balances manually or through recorded transactions.

LOG TRANSACTIONS QUICKLY AND EASILY
Record income, expenses, transfers, and adjustments between accounts in seconds. Add descriptions, timestamps, and custom labels to keep every transaction organized and easy to find. Need to catch up? Bulk-add multiple transactions at once.

SET BUDGETS AND REACH YOUR FINANCIAL GOALS
Define spending budgets and savings goals that match your lifestyle. Track your progress over time and stay motivated as you work toward financial milestones — whether that is paying off debt, building an emergency fund, or saving for something special.

COLLABORATE WITH SHARED SPACES
Recontrol makes it easy to manage money together. Create shared Spaces to track finances with a partner, roommates, family members, or a team. Invite members, create custom roles, and assign granular permissions so everyone has the right level of access — without compromising privacy or control.

ORGANIZE WITH CUSTOM LABELS
Create your own categories and tag transactions with custom labels. Whether it is groceries, rent, subscriptions, entertainment, or travel, labels give you a clear picture of exactly where your money goes each month.

MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT
Select your preferred currency from a wide range of international currencies. All your financial data is displayed in the format that makes the most sense to you.

SECURE BY DESIGN
Your financial data deserves strong protection. Recontrol encrypts all data in transit using TLS. Your login credentials are stored securely on your device using Android Keystore. Enable multi-factor authentication for an extra layer of account security.

YOUR DATA BELONGS TO YOU
Recontrol does not sell your personal data. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics that follow you across apps. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time — directly from the app or through our website. You can also request written confirmation that your data has been deleted.

SIMPLE, CLEAN, AND FOCUSED
No ads. No clutter. No unnecessary complexity. Recontrol is designed to be the simplest, most effective way to manage your personal finances. A clean interface helps you spend less time in the app and more time making smart decisions with your money.

WHO IS RECONTROL FOR?
Whether you are a student tracking expenses, a couple managing a household budget, a freelancer monitoring income and costs, or anyone who wants a clear view of their financial life — Recontrol is built for you.

KEY FEATURES AT A GLANCE:
• Track accounts: assets, liabilities, income, expenses, equity
• Record and categorize transactions with labels and descriptions
• Set and monitor budgets and savings goals
• Create shared Spaces for collaborative finance management
• Assign roles with granular, permission-based access control
• Multi-currency support with customizable display
• Multi-factor authentication for account security
• Full data deletion available anytime
• No ads, no data selling, no tracking

Recontrol — take control of your financial future.
```
