```
Recontrol puts you in control of your money. Track every account, record every transaction, and see your complete financial picture — whether you manage your finances solo or with a partner, family, or team.

TRACK ALL YOUR ACCOUNTS IN ONE PLACE
Create accounts for everything: bank accounts, credit cards, cash, investments, loans, and more. Organize them by type — assets, liabilities, income, expense, and equity — and always know exactly where your money stands.

LOG TRANSACTIONS WITH EASE
Record income, expenses, and transfers between accounts in seconds. Add descriptions, timestamps, and custom labels to keep every transaction organized and easy to find. Bulk-add transactions when you need to catch up.

SET BUDGETS AND FINANCIAL GOALS
Define spending limits and savings targets that work for your lifestyle. Monitor your progress and stay motivated as you work toward your financial milestones.

COLLABORATE IN SHARED SPACES

ORGANIZE WITH CUSTOM LABELS
Tag transactions with your own categories. Whether it is groceries, rent, subscriptions, or travel, labels help you understand exactly where your money goes.

MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT
Choose your preferred currency and see all your financial data displayed the way that makes sense to you.

BUILT WITH SECURITY IN MIND
Your data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Credentials are stored securely on your device using platform-native secure storage. Multi-factor authentication is available to add an extra layer of protection to your account.

YOUR DATA, YOUR CONTROL
Recontrol does not sell your data. We do not use advertising trackers. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from the app. Request written confirmation of deletion whenever you need it.

DESIGNED FOR SIMPLICITY
A clean, intuitive interface means you spend less time managing your app and more time managing your money. No clutter, no unnecessary complexity — just the tools you need.

Whether you are paying off debt, building savings, splitting expenses, or simply keeping track of where your money goes, Recontrol gives you the clarity and control you need to make smarter financial decisions.

Download Recontrol today and take control of your financial future.
```
