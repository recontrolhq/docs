---
title: "Delete your data"
description: "How to permanently delete your Recontrol account and all associated data."
---

<Warning>
Deletion is permanent and irreversible. We do not maintain backups. Once your deletion request is processed, your data cannot be recovered by you or by us under any circumstances.
</Warning>

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following is permanently removed from our systems:

- Your user profile (email address, display name, phone number)
- All financial accounts you created across any space
- All transactions you recorded across any space
- All spaces you created, and all data within them
- Your membership in all shared spaces
- Your role assignments
- Your settings and currency preferences
- All authentication tokens and session data

Nothing is retained for business purposes after deletion.

## Method 1: Delete from within the app (recommended)

In-app deletion is immediate.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the **Settings** tab at the bottom of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Delete Account">
    Scroll to the bottom of Settings and tap **Delete Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Read the confirmation prompt and tap **Delete Account** to confirm. Your account and all data are deleted immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you are the sole admin of a shared space, you must transfer ownership to another member or delete the space before account deletion can complete. The app will guide you through this.
</Note>

## Method 2: Email request

If you cannot access the app, email us to request deletion.

**Send to:** [support@recontrol.money](mailto:support@recontrol.money?subject=Account%20deletion%20request)
**Subject:** `Account deletion request`

Include:
- The email address on your Recontrol account
- A clear statement that you want your account and all associated data permanently deleted

We may verify your identity before processing to protect your data from unauthorised deletion. Email requests are processed within **30 days** of receipt.

## Processing times

| Method | Timeframe |
|---|---|
| In-app deletion | Immediate |
| Email request | Within 30 days of receipt |

## Data retained after deletion

After your account is deleted, we may retain minimal records only where required by applicable Indian law — for example, records subject to a court order or law enforcement process. We will never retain data beyond what is legally mandated, and never for business purposes.

## Written confirmation

You may request written confirmation that your data has been deleted. Email [support@recontrol.money](mailto:support@recontrol.money) after deletion is complete and we will provide it within a reasonable timeframe.
