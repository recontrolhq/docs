## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your email or Apple ID. A personal space is created for you automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your financial accounts">
    Create accounts for each asset, liability, income source, and spending category you want to track. Set opening balances to start from where you are today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record transactions">
    Every time money moves, add a transaction. Recontrol records both sides — from which account, to which account — and keeps every balance mathematically correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Built for clarity, not complexity

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="No bank connections" icon="lock">
    Recontrol never asks for bank credentials. All data is entered manually — you stay in full control.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No ads, ever" icon="ban">
    There is no ad network, no data broker, no monetisation through your information. You pay nothing and nothing is sold.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your data stays in India" icon="server">
    All data is stored on servers in Mumbai, India (AWS ap-south-1). It never leaves Indian infrastructure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Private by default" icon="eye-slash">
    No analytics SDK, no crash-reporting SDK, no third-party tracking of any kind runs inside the app.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
